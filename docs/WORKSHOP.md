

3 hours workshop with hands-on exercises in a sandbox environment.

# Workshop Agenda

9:00 AM - 9:15 AM: Introduction to Chaos Engineering
- Overview of Chaos Engineering principles
- Benefits of Chaos Engineering in cloud-native environments
9:15 AM - 9:30 AM: Understanding the System Architecture & Steady State
- Overview of the system architecture
- Defining steady state and resilience hypotheses
9:30 AM - 11:30 AM: Hands-on Chaos Experiments
- Lab 1 - Manual Chaos Experiments: Injecting failures into Kubernetes microservices and observing results
- Lab 2 - AI-Driven Chaos Experiments: Using AI tools to generate chaos experiments and observe results
- Lab 3 - AI-Driven Resilience Improvement: Using AI tools to investigate failures and improve resilience
11:30 AM - 12:00 PM: Sharing Results and Lessons Learned
- Group discussion on findings and real-world applications
